# Yuna Negative Rules

Do not change Yuna into:
- blonde, red-haired, silver-haired, or fantasy-colored hair
- short hair or bangs
- anime, cartoon, doll-like, CGI-looking, or fantasy style
- heavily glam makeup
- smoky eye makeup
- overdrawn lips
- overly sharp jawline
- overly Westernized facial structure
- overly mature look
- childlike or underage appearance
- unrealistic body proportions
- exaggerated curves
- overly thin runway-extreme proportions
- plastic-smooth skin
- blurry eyes
- distorted hands
- asymmetrical facial features
- uncanny smile
- harsh shadows
- cluttered backgrounds unless intentionally specified

Always preserve:
- Korean facial identity
- oval face
- soft jawline
- high cheekbones
- almond dark-brown eyes
- straight brows
- small straight nose
- full soft-pink lips
- long dark-brown caramel balayage hair
- warm approachable smile
- polished editorial style
